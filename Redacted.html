Redacted!

Blackpool & The Fylde College 2023 Game Jam

Redacted is a simple spy themed platforming game where you jump and zipline your way across a level to reach multiple goals.

On this Game Jam I worked on a team with 2 other people, Kate Faulkner and Richard Ferguson, Kate served as the as lead asset designer, Richard as the level designer/builder and I acted as the game's programmer.

In the game you must retrieve a folder from one side of the map and the bring it to the postbox at the other side, to do this you must use your trust zipline launcher and spring seat to boost and slide across the map, avoiding turrents along the way.

The game is relatively self-explanatory, and is available below.
