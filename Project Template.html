Brief Description

I am a 21 Year Old Msc. By Research student at Lancaster University, where I am working under Professor Tracy Hall and Doctor Saad Ezzini to develop Natural Language Processing based applications to aid developers in working with user feedback.

When I'm not working on my University research, I love to develop indie games. In the few years since I have started game development I have already published one title to Steam, with another being released this december under the banner I've given my hobby projects: "Mischievous Mayhem".

As stressful as releasing your work into the world can be, I believe that the rewards of learning from feedback and observations of user behaviour are the best possible way to improve and push yourself ever so slightly toward reaching your ultimate goal, to me, that is to become a succesful developer with a multitude of projects I can be proud of.
